Warhol's Career

Skills/Talents
Published Works
Velvet-Underground

Not only is Warhol credited for the album artwork, he also is the only credited producer of the album. The band appeared on the "Exploding Plastic Inevitable," a series of multimedia events.

Marilyn Monroe

This piece is one of Warhol's most notable. The work was completed weeks after Monroe's death. It was done using the screenprinting technique, a technique popularized by Warhol.

Mao Zedong

Another of Warhol's screen printed works. It was done after reading that Mao was considered the most famous person in the world at the time.

Where Has Warhol Lived?
Carnegie Institute of Technology/NYC

Warhol has traveled the world, but he has mainly called New York City his home. He was born on August 6, 1928 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania but moved to New York City after receiving his Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in 1949 from Carnegie Institute of Technology.

Collaborations

The most well-known collaboration was likely between Warhol and young artist Jean-Michel Basquiat, a New York native known for mixing mediums in a new and innovative way.
